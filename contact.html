Helpful Information

Answers to some common questions about contacting Pioneer Works Limited.

What is the best way to contact Pioneer Works Limited?

You can call us on 020 4510 601, email info@pioneerworks.co.nz, or use the general contact form on this page.

Which email should I use for quote requests?

Quote requests should be sent through the dedicated Request Quote page or emailed to quotes@pioneerworks.co.nz.

Which areas do you service?

Pioneer Works Limited services Auckland, Waikato and Bay of Plenty, subject to project size, location and availability.

Can suppliers or businesses contact you?

Yes. Suppliers and businesses are welcome to use the general contact form and select the most suitable enquiry type.

How quickly will you respond?

We aim to respond as soon as reasonably possible during normal business hours.

Can I contact you outside business hours?

Yes. You can submit the online form or send an email at any time, and we will respond during our contact hours.
