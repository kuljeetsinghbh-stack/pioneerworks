Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Pioneer Works Limited respects your privacy and is committed to handling personal information responsibly and in accordance with applicable New Zealand privacy requirements.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, why we collect it, how it may be used, and how you can contact us regarding your personal information.

1. Information We May Collect

When you contact us or request a quotation, we may collect:

  • Your full name.
  • Your phone number.
  • Your email address.
  • Your suburb or property address, when provided.
  • The region in which the work is required.
  • Your preferred contact method.
  • Details about your project or enquiry.
  • Photographs or documents you voluntarily provide.
  • General technical information relating to website use.

2. How We Collect Information

We may collect information when you:

  • Submit a contact or quotation form.
  • Contact us by phone or email.
  • Communicate with us through social media.
  • Provide information during a site visit or project discussion.
  • Use this website.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to enquiries.
  • Assess project requirements.
  • Prepare or discuss quotations.
  • Arrange site visits or appointments.
  • Communicate about current or proposed work.
  • Provide customer service.
  • Maintain business and project records.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent personal information.

Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary, including with service providers, professional advisers, subcontractors or authorities where required by law.

5. Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information against unauthorised access, misuse, disclosure, alteration or loss.

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

6. Retention of Information

We may retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to enquiries, manage projects, maintain business records, resolve disputes or meet legal and accounting requirements.

7. Cookies and Website Analytics

This website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve website performance, understand visitor behaviour and provide a better browsing experience.

You can usually manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Services and Links

This website may contain links to third-party services such as Google, social media platforms or mapping services.

Pioneer Works Limited is not responsible for the privacy practices, security or content of third-party websites.

9. Accessing or Correcting Your Information

You may contact us to request access to personal information we hold about you, or to ask that inaccurate information be corrected.

We may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

10. Privacy Questions or Complaints

Privacy questions or concerns can be sent to:

info@pioneerworks.co.nz
